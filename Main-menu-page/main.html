How to Play:

Select Your Mode:
Choose between a 2x2 or 3x3 Rubik's Cube to start.

Controls:
Mouse/Touch: Click and drag a face to rotate.
Keyboard: Use arrow keys or designated keys.
Hand Gesture: Use webcam to control the cube.

Timing & Moves:
Solve the cube as fast as possible while tracking your moves for a high score.

Menu Buttons:
Settings: Adjust volume, language, resolution, brightness, and speed.
Rubik's Color: Customize cube colors.
Stats: View your total solves, best time, and average time.
Credits: See developer and contributor info.
About Us: Learn about the creators.

Additional Features:
Reset your settings anytime using the Reset button.
