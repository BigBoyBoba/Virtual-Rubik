How to Play:

Select Your Mode:
Choose the size of your Rubik's Cube (2x2 or 3x3)

Controls & Movement:
Mouse/Touch: Click and drag a face of the cube to rotate it.
Keyboard (if supported): Use arrow keys or designated keys to rotate specific layers.
Hand Gesture Control (using camera): Use your hand movements detected by the webcam to interact with and rotate the Rubik’s Cube.

Timing & Moves:
When you start the game, a timer will begin. Try to solve the cube as quickly as possible.
Your moves are tracked, so aim to use the fewest moves for a high score.

Using Menus:
Settings: Adjust volume, language, resolution, brightness, and rotation speed by clicking the Settings button (located on the right).
Rubik’s Cube Size: Choose between 2x2 or 3x3 cube sizes.
Stats: View your gameplay statistics such as Total Solves, Best Time, and Average Time.
Credits: Access information about the developers and contributors.
Song Selection: You can also choose a background song to play while solving the puzzle.

Additional Features:
Customize the cube's colors through the Rubik's Color Settings in the Settings menu. To reset your settings, click the Reset button.
